Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

LootBox is designed to function as a local-first application. This means that your data stays on your device and is not required to be transmitted to external servers for normal use.

Data Storage

Loot lists, settings, and app state are stored locally on your device.

LootBox does not require an account and does not rely on cloud storage to operate.

Data Collection

LootBox does not collect personal data during normal use.

Data Sharing

LootBox does not sell or share personal data.

Changes to This Policy
