FAQ

Do I need an account?

No. LootBox is designed to work without accounts or identity management.

Does LootBox work offline?

Yes. LootBox is designed as a local-first app with no required online account.

What is the difference between LootBox and LootBox Events?

LootBox is game themed. LootBox Events is focused on raffles, classrooms, meetings, and general event use.

Where is the Windows version?

The Windows button above always points to the latest production desktop release.

Is iPhone available?

Not yet. iOS release is planned for a future launch stage.

Privacy-first by design

LootBox does not require accounts, hosted profiles, or identity management.
